Designer Jobs

Job Responsibilities:

  • Development of new design concepts and ideas via graphic that appeals to target audiences while adhering to brand guidelines.
  • Assist the Marketing & Communications in company marketing campaigns.
  • Work closely with the Marketing & Communications team to develop materials such as posters, banners, backdrops, and other design needs.
  • Support design chapter projects to bring user experience or processes inside the team to the next level.
  • Ensure that the quality of implementation of product meets design requirements.
  • Execute and lead across all phases of the design process: research, conceptual design, prototyping, validating, production ready designs, usability testing and implementation.
  • Collaborate with multiple professionals in your cross-functional team (squad) to reach your common goal of improving the product and user experience daily.

Required Experience:

  • At least 3 to 5 years of experience in product design.
  • 5+ years (for senior position), of experience in product/interaction design in a consumer-focused, high performance organization.
  • Extensive experience in designing and shipping for Android and iOS apps, and working with cross-platform requirements.
  • Experience working with Sketch, prototyping and design tools such as InVision and Photoshop required.
  • Consideration for long-term sustainability in all design decisions, with a focus on engineering feasibility and business impact.
  • Deep expertise in visual and system design.
  • Experience creating wireframes, user flows, sitemaps.

  • A dynamic and challenging working environment
  • An extra steep learning curve
  • Responsibility from day one in a fast growing and global company
  • A vibrant and international team with a diverse background
  • Regular company and team events
