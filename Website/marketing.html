MARKETING

There is an opportunity to join a leading professional services firm in a Marketing & Communications Manager role. This position will work closely with the Director of Business Development and Marketing to oversee and implement the strategic marketing plans and ensure that they are aligned with the firm's overall objectives. This position will also be required to work closely with lawyers and industry leaders to take the lead in marketing initiatives, from inception to execution.

The ideal candidate should possess a can-do attitude and be passionate about making a difference, not just in the marketing capacity but also to the wider team.

Requirements

  • Candidates must possess a Degree in relevant field.
  • Exhibit leadership qualities, preferably with min 3 years of experience in a Managerial role.
  • Proficient in utilising digital and traditional marketing platforms as marketing tools e.g. Social Media outlets, trade publications, seminars.
  • Proficient in business writing and communication skills.
  • Ability to handle multiple projects concurrently and excellent in time management.

What you'll need to succeed

  • Ability to work with management to achieve the firm's overall strategy using the different marketing channels.
  • Demonstrates a strong command of digital marketing tools and technology.
  • Responsible for the management and performance of the firm's website and social media strategy.
  • Ability to project manage and co-ordinate logistic support for internal events, including production of eDM, onsite support and social media event postings.
  • Management of client database, for the purpose of marketing campaigns and client communications.
  • Has the leadership skills to manage and supervise the Client Communications Executive.
  • Work with various business units to roll out marketing campaigns and events; track results and provide analysis
